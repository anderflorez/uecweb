About

Unlimited Companies is composed of three dynamic South Florida businesses. They are Unlimited Electrical Contractors, Unlimited Custom Pools and Spa’s and Unlimited Electronic Environments. These three corporations were brought together under one umbrella by the owner, Mitchell R. Pagerey.

  • Unlimited Electrical Contractors engages in commercial building and residential projects and provides prompt electric repair and trouble shooting services for large and small customers.
  • Unlimited Electronic Environments develops sophisticated controls for lighting, air conditioning, sound, video surveillance, alarms and amazing theaters for homes and businesses.
  • Unlimited Custom Pools & Spas specializes in designing and building backyard pool and spa installations that are carefully customized to suit each customer’s needs and dreams.
