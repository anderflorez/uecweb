Unlimited Electrical Contractors, Corp. (UEC) is a full-service electrical contracting company founded in 1999 and headquartered in Pompano Beach, Florida. We specialize in all aspects of the electrical industry ranging from contract, retro-fitting, site lighting, trenching, low-voltage systems, fire alarm installation, service and much more. We procure all of our materials from the major electrical supply houses, while maintaining an excellent credit standing with them.

UEC is state licensed, insured, and bonded. We have successfully performed electrical installations for Community Parks, Motorsports Parks, Schools, Hi-rise Condo / Apartment Buildings, Restaurants, Hotels, Commercial Buildings, Fitness Centers, Interior Build-outs and more.

We pride ourselves on high performance and have all the resources to outperform our competition. Comprised of an experienced team of professionals, UEC is committed to quality and customer satisfaction. We have established a proven track record of consistently completing projects on-time, and have ample bonding capacity to back us up.

UEC emphasizes safety and equal opportunity programs. Moreover, we are a Drug-Free Work Place and enjoy a 0.95 worker’s compensation Experience Mod Rating (EMR).
