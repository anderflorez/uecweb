Unlimited Electronic Environments creates advanced home theaters that set the highest standard for excellence. Entertainment, light, room temperature, humidity and multi-level communication capacities are developed in collaboration with the owner’s needs and wishes. A primary achievement is always ease of use and high functionality.

In short, the theater experience we provide is exhilarating and the controls we design to harness complex electronics are a pleasure to use.

The features of our systems allow for a wide range of functions that transcend anything previously available: sound for many rooms (as many as you wish), lighting controls not just in the home theater but inside and outside the home or office, state-of-the-art sound and video management that incorporate every thing from telephone to jukebox utilities.

Standing first in the field of creative use of modern electronics in the home, Unlimited Electronic Environments delivers the highest and best utilization of state-of-the-art touch pads and and hand held remotes.

Our commitment to excellence extends into the Marine industry. We design and install sophisticated audio/video systems from full automation on Mega Yachts down to center console fishing boats.

Our Mission
  • Develop superior systems that are dependable, affordable and able to seamlessly grow as new applications become desirable and needed.
  • Meld sophisticated software and hardware into one integrated piece that is easy to operate.
  • Give clients a full understanding of how to easily and simply use an integrated system, including home security features.
Our Values
  • The best components and design at the best value.
  • Create systems that are easily adaptable to future advances.
  • Make it all operator friendly.
Our Certifications

Programmer Michel St. Amand has accomplished a Crestron Certification. This is a top honor in our indusrty. Unlimited Electronic Environments continues to expand it’s high quality service in the South Florida lighting controls industry by certifying it’s professionals. Michel St Amand received Commercial Lighting Controls CLC-C certification during training classes in West Palm Beach, FL, and is now authorized to design, install and maintain Crestron Lighting Control Systems.

The intensive three-day curriculum teaches Crestron Lighting Control System design, installation and programming. The class provides students with an overview of Crestron commercial lighting control systems, including centralized, distributed, room building and enterprise solutions. The Crestron Lighting Control Software provides system configuration and programming demonstrations.
